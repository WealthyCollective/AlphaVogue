Our Mission

The trends being sold to us
are not built for us.

Alpha Vogue was founded at age 11 with one question: why does everything marketed to our generation — the drinks, the food, the beauty products — seem designed to hook us, not help us? We're done being passive consumers of habits that harm us. This platform exists to call it out, decode the noise, and spotlight the clean, conscious alternatives worth our attention.

What we drink

Ultra-processed beverages, hidden sugars, energy drinks targeting kids — we're exposing what's in the can.

What we eat

Ingredient transparency, food dyes, ultra-processed snacks — and the clean-label brands doing it differently.

What we put on our skin

The skincare-to-school pipeline is real. We're questioning what belongs on an 11-year-old's face.

What we scroll

Trend cycles engineered for addiction. We're building media literacy from the inside.
